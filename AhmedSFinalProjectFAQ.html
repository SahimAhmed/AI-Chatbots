What is a chatbot?
An AI chatbot is an artifical intelligence based computer program which allows interactions with humans whether it is by voice or text. These chatbots can understand and comprehend user requests and needs by porviding a relevant answer.
What information sources will these chatbots use?
These chatbots are connected to the internet and the world wide web so given permission by the creators and any other pieces of data stored into these chatbots. When the chatbot is given a prompt by the user, it will then use all the data it has available to it to provide the user with a sufficient response.
What else can AI chatbots do other than provide answers and responses to a users request?
Many people associate AI chatbots with things like chatgpt where most people simply give the AI a question for them to recieve a response. However, AI chatbots are more than that since thing slike Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant, and more. Therefore, all the functions of these applications like playing music, making phone calls/texts, booking trips or plans, and etc is all things AI chatbots as a whole can do.
